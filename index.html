✦ WHO AM I?

Hello! I'm Sahil Patel, a multidisciplinary data science enthusiast and aspiring data analyst based in India. I'm deeply passionate about transforming data into impactful insights, and if you're curious, you can check out my projects on GitHub and connect with me on LinkedIn.

I've had the opportunity to work with companies in different industries, and I'm always ready for new and exciting challenges.

With a background in BCA Data Science from SRM Institute of Science and Technology, I've developed a strong foundation in data-driven problem solving using tools like Power BI, Python, SQL, and Excel. My academic journey and hands-on experiences have allowed me to explore various facets of data science—from crafting dynamic dashboards that visualize complex datasets, to developing machine learning models that provide actionable insights. I find great satisfaction in uncovering patterns, trends, and narratives hidden within data, and using them to drive smarter decisions.

Beyond academics, I've taken part in national and international challenges, including the NASA International Space Apps Challenge, where I collaborated with like-minded innovators on space and earth-related data problems. I've also completed a data science internship with Cognifyz, where I applied analytical techniques to real-world scenarios, strengthening both my technical and collaborative skills. I'm continuously learning, experimenting, and looking for new opportunities to grow, contribute, and create impact in the field of data.
